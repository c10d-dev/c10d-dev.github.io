This privacy policy applies to the **Multiplications** mobile application (hereby referred to as the "Application") for mobile devices that was created by c10d (hereby referred to as "Service Provider"). This service is intended for use "AS IS".

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed with a "Privacy by Design" approach. 

- **Personal Data:** The Application does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical location.
- **Account Creation:** You do not need to create an account to use the Application.
- **Device Information:** The Application does not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or any sensitive device sensors.

## 2. Local Storage and Data Persistence

The Application uses local storage (via `shared_preferences`) on your device to enhance your experience. This data stays on your device and is never uploaded to any external server.


## 3. Third-Party Services

The Application does not include any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data brokers. It does not use cookies or tracking pixels.

## 4. Children's Privacy

Because the Application do not collect any personal data from any user, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or any other age). 

## 5. Security

The security of your information is important to us. Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on the security of your physical device and operating system. We do not transmit any data over the internet.

## 6. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all information associated with the Application at any time by:
1. Clearing the Application's cache/data in your device settings.
2. Uninstalling the Application.

## 7. Permissions

The Application requires no special permissions (like Location, Contacts, or Storage access) on Android or iOS.
